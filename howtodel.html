Delete Your Account

This page explains exactly how to permanently delete your PRSquad account and associated data.

Delete directly in the app

  1. Open PRSquad and sign in to your account.
  2. Go to your Profile page.
  3. Tap the hamburger menu in the top-right corner.
  4. Select Settings.
  5. Tap Delete Account.
  6. Confirm deletion to permanently remove your account.

Can not access the app?

If you cannot sign in, you can request account deletion by email:

support@prsquad.app

Use the email address linked to your PRSquad account so we can verify and process your request securely.

What gets deleted

  • Your account profile and login credentials.
  • Your workout logs and personal training history.
  • Your social posts and in-app activity tied to your account.
  • Your badge and milestone progress.

Data retention details

  • Account deletion requests are processed as soon as possible, usually within 7 days.
  • Required transactional or legal records may be retained for up to 30 days where needed for compliance and fraud prevention.
  • Backups are automatically purged on a rolling schedule and are fully removed within 30 days.
